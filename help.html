How to Load your Gcash Beep Card


  1. Open the Beep™ App.
  2. Click on the registered card or Load Unregistered Card
  3. Enter the amount you want to reload or choose from existing choices.
  4. Click the Continue button twice.
  5. Click “GCash”
  6. Confirm your payment with GCash.
  7. Click the Back to Merchant button.
  8. Follow the instructions on the screen to tap your beep card on the back of your smartphone.

Safety Tips and Reminders


  1. Stay Behind the Yellow Line – Always stand behind the yellow safety line while waiting for the train to avoid accidents.
  2. Let Passengers Exit First – Allow people to get off the train before boarding to ensure a smooth and orderly flow.
  3. Hold Onto Handrails – If you're standing, hold onto handrails or poles to keep your balance, especially when the train moves suddenly.
  4. Mind the Gap – Watch your step when entering or exiting the train to avoid slipping or tripping.
  5. Keep an Eye on Your Belongings – Be aware of your surroundings and secure your bags, wallets, and phones to prevent theft.
  6. Avoid Blocking Doors – Do not stand too close to or lean on the doors, as they need to close properly for the train to operate.
