Welcome to the LRT-1 station

At LRT-1, we provide a fast, reliable, and affordable way to travel across Metro Manila. Our line runs from Baclaran in Pasay City to Fernando Poe Jr. (Roosevelt) in Quezon City, connecting key business districts, shopping centers, universities, and residential areas. With 20 stations, we make daily commutes easier and more accessible for thousands of passengers.

Our stations are strategically located to serve major destinations like EDSA, Gil Puyat, Central Terminal, Monumento, and Balintawak. Whether you're heading to work, school, or a leisure trip, LRT-1 helps you get there faster and more conveniently. We also offer various ticketing options, including stored-value Beep cards, for a smoother and more efficient journey.

We are committed to improving public transportation, with ongoing upgrades and expansions to serve you better. Travel with us and experience a seamless commute across Metro Manila!
